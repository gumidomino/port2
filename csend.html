THE SILENCE
school project
art school of buda
2020

short story illustration:
AR interactive abstract illustrations, layout and text editing


---------------------------------
A Csend / The Silence by Béla Balázs is a short melancholic story from 1908. The meaning is rather controversial, some says it is about death, others think it is more of about growing up. To reflect the ambiguous meaning, my aim was to create illustrations with little story telling, to leave the analysis to the reader.
​ My illustrations are short, 4-second animations that can be revealed with a mobile app. I made some key elements that are easily recogniseable and connect to the story literally but I intentionally wanted to keep the vaugeness of the narrative.
