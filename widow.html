WIDOW
school project
university of the arts london
2021

task:
Create a typographic poster that advertises a graphic design talk on one of six given topics (orphan, river, rug, bleed, widow, body) at the university to first year students. Investigate and express the meaning of the topic in a variety of ways.

---------------------------------

a widow is a woman who has lost her spouse by death and has not married again.

widow is a last word or short last line of a paragraph falling at the top of a page or column and considered undesirable.

the base of my concept is taking the strongest elements of both definitions:
_losing someone who completes us
_being the odd line

for my poster i created a set of characters that are based on simple pixel letters but all of them miss their top and bottom row of their pixels, but all contain the top of the following and the bottom of the previous character.

reducing the basic pixel characters

the letters complete each other and are difficult to identify standing alone by themselves.

poster for a fictional talk/workshop at the university
