DOOG
school project
art school of buda
2020

package design:
illustration, package design, 3D model


---------------------------------
DOOG is a multivitamin for dogs. The name indicates the pure nature of dogs as it is GOOD backwards. The package aims to be cheerful, playful, thus imitates the head of a dog and it opens like its mouth. After several hand-cut prototypes, I modelled the whole package in Cinema 4D.
