poster:
set of posters for a fictional festival


---------------------------------
Neon festival is a fictional urban festival meant to set in Budapest, Hungary. Gastronomy, Dance, Art and Theatre are the four main topics the event is mainly about. Our task was to create cohesive posters for each field.
My concept was to take basic household elements, photograph and distort them into something else that could (not necessarily) revoke the feeling of the four art fields.
